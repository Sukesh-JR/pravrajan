About Us - why are we doing this

We have come up with this website having the vision of Making Bharat Atmanirbhar. We provide a common platform for migrant workers as well as employers.

As Patrick Kingsley once said, “For a start, people who traveled for so many miles through such horrific conditions in order to find work cannot accurately be portrayed as lazy benefit-scroungers”, there is a stereotype that migrant workers are lazy and want to earn money without much hard work, but this completely wrong.

Moreover, the plight of the migrant workers in India during the lockdown has even reached the attention of UN.

Pervasive challenges remain for hundreds-of-thousands of migrant workers in India whose lives were upended by the sudden lockdown across the country, in response to the threat of the coronavirus pandemic, said the UN human rights chief on 2nd April 2020.

The plight of migrant workers in India is incredibly sad faced.

This step that we have taken is a small effort for the betterment of migrant workers.
